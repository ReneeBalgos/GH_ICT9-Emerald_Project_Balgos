Fires

Fires can happen anytime, anywhere and if you’re not careful it can happen at your house. There are MANY different reasons as to why a fire can from. From chargers, candles, stoves, gas leaks, carelessness, and so much more. Anyone and everyone can be a victim of fires.

Why is it important to prevent this. Well, first off you can loose not only your house but the houses around you. It can cause lost of lives and belongings as well as livelihood. If you don’t die from the fire, it can cause trauma and pain not only physically but also emotionally. Fires can cause a lot of problems and distruction that’s why we need to be careful and mindfulof our surroundings.


What are things we can do to prevent this??
Keep lit candles away from objects that easily catch fire. By keeping candles or open fires near easily fire catching objects can be a HUGE fire hazard. If you put candles near easy to light objects like curtains, it may get caught or touch the flame thus causing it to grow into a bigger, harder to control fire. From a little "harmless" flame, it can turn into a huge disaster that may end in the lost of lives and belongings.
Do not overload your electrical circuits If you overload your circuits, it can cause the circuit wiring to overheat or short circuit and cause a fire. Especially if the circuit breaker doesn't automatically shut down when needed.
Kerosene and gas stoves should be checked for leakage and regularly cleaned. By doing this, it can prevent dangerous leaks and keep everyone safe. If there is a gas leakage and a bulb or a fire is lit in the room, it can cause an explosion that can make a big fire and kills a lot of people. An easy way to check for leaks is by using soapy water. If there are bubbles, there is a leak and it is best to call for help.
Check wries regularly and best by a professional Faulty electrical wires is one of the leading cause of house fires in the Philippines. Improper connection of wires can lead to a deadly fire. By getting them check by a professional can greatly lessen the chances of a house fire. Actually just by getting them installed by professionals can decrease chances of house fires.
Proper discarding of cigarette butts This is also one of the leading cause of fires in the Philippines. By improperly dissposing of cigarette butts may lead it to mix with paper and plastic materials, if the cigarette butt is still hot, it can lead to a fire. So be sure to crush and throw them properly.
